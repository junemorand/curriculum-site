From Operations to Systems Thinking — Welcome Post

This is the welcome post for this site. The full essay lives on the About page. This post is a shorter version for the archive.

This site documents a 30-month path from operations and IT into systems-level software engineering. The curriculum is structured around WGU's B.S. in Software Engineering (C# track) with independent depth work in algorithms, databases, systems programming, and the Shopify ecosystem.

Posts will cover phase-by-phase progress, the Shopify app build from requirements through deployment, book notes, and teaching observations from working through this material with students.

The full curriculum — phases, courses, books, projects, and transfer credits — is on the Curriculum page.
